The University Library

This library complex is the centre for studies at the UMaT campus. It also contain an Information Technology (IT) unit where students can access the internet for research and project purposes. It has three floor compartments with each containing about 50 seats. The UMaT library also houses past project work of students to aid easy referencing by current students. The library is situated at the centre of the university campus. In this project, a 3D model of this building will be simulated
